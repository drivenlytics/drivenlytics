## Locked Section Order

1. **Hero** — "Performance-driven marketing that delivers results" + CTA (Book a call / Learn more)
2. **About** — Lawrence's background, methodology, why Drivenlytics exists
3. **Services Offered** — Overview of all service tiers (Launch Ready, Brand Foundation Kit, Custom Retainer, content, SEO, builds, email, ads, automation)
4. **How We Work** — 3–4 step process (Discovery → Strategy → Execution → Results)
5. **Case Studies** — 2–3 results-focused client outcomes with metrics (e.g., "Grew SaaS signup rate 340% in 90 days")
6. **Pricing / Service Tiers** — Clear breakdown of what's included at each level
7. **FAQ** — Common objections ("Do you work with my industry?" "What's the timeline?" "How much does a custom retainer cost?")
8. **CTA** — Final "Let's talk about your project" + booking link
9. **Footer** — Contact, links, social, legal
