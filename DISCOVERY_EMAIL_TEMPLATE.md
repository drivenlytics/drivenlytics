# Discovery Interview Email Template

Use this when conducting discovery via email. Send to client, request responses, compile into DISCOVERY.md for the project.

---

**Subject:** Quick Discovery Questions for Your Website Build

Hi [Client Name],

To make sure we build exactly what your business needs, I need answers to 5 quick questions. These will guide everything from architecture to copy to integrations.

Reply with your answers directly in this email. Nothing fancy—just your honest takes.

---

## 5 Discovery Questions

### 1. North Star
**What is the single most important outcome for this site?**

The ONE thing that matters most. Everything else is secondary. (e.g., "Generate qualified leads for demo calls")

*Your answer:*

---

### 2. Integrations
**Which external services are needed? Are credentials ready?**

Examples: Vercel (hosting), GitHub (code), CMS (content), Zapier (automation), email platform, payment processor, API connections.

Which do you need? Do you have API keys / credentials ready?

*Your answer:*

---

### 3. Source of Truth
**Where does the site's content live?**

Is content hardcoded on the site? In WordPress? In markdown files? In a database? Airtable? Who updates it?

*Your answer:*

---

### 4. Delivery Payload
**What does 'done' look like? Where does the site live and how do visitors reach it?**

- Custom domain or subdomain?
- Vercel, WordPress, or custom hosting?
- When should it launch?
- Any staging/preview environment needed?

*Your answer:*

---

### 5. Behavioral Rules
**How should the site behave?**

- Brand voice/tone (professional, casual, playful, etc.)
- Animation style (minimal, moderate, lots)
- Performance targets (fastest possible, standard, doesn't matter)
- Accessibility needs (AA, AAA, standard)
- Things you want to avoid (no autoplay, no pop-ups, etc.)

*Your answer:*

---

Reply with these 5 answers, and we'll lock in the architecture and move forward.
