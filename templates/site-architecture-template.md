## Core 4 Sections (Required on Every Site)

Every site needs these four sections—they directly support your conversion goal:

### 1. Hero
- **What it does:** Opens with your main promise + calls visitor to action immediately
- **Copy focus:** One clear hook (what you do + who it's for)
- **Visual:** Hero image, video, or graphic that reinforces the promise

### 2. About / Trust
- **What it does:** Answers "Why should I trust this?"
- **For services:** Your background, methodology, why you do this work
- **For products:** Why the product exists, what problem it solves
- **For courses:** Instructor credentials + student outcomes

### 3. Main Value Delivery
- **What it does:** Explains HOW your offer works or what the customer gets
- **For services:** Service description + process overview
- **For products:** Features + how customers benefit
- **For courses:** What they'll learn + how it's structured
- **For ecommerce:** Product details + what's included

### 4. Call-to-Action (CTA)
- **What it does:** Directs the visitor to the next step
- **Examples:** Schedule a call, Start Free Trial, Add to Cart, Subscribe
- **Important:** Should appear multiple times throughout the site, with increasing urgency

## Conditional Sections (Based on Your Offer Type)

Add sections based on what you're selling. **Skip sections that don't apply to your offer.**

### For Service Offers
- **Case Studies / Results** — Show 2–3 detailed client outcomes with metrics (revenue gained, time saved, problems solved)
- **Social Proof / Testimonials** — Client quotes + logos (builds trust in quality)
- **Process / How We Work** — Step-by-step explanation of your engagement (discovery, execution, results)
- **FAQ** — Address common objections ("How long does this take?" "Do you work with [my industry]?")

### For Product / SaaS Offers
- **Features** — Core capabilities (show 4–6, not 20)
- **Pricing** — Transparent cost + what's included at each tier
- **Social Proof** — User testimonials, logos, user count
- **FAQ** — Address product-specific questions ("Is there a free trial?" "What integrations do you support?")

### For Course / Membership Offers
- **Curriculum / What's Inside** — Breakdown of modules, lessons, bonus materials
- **Social Proof / Success Stories** — Student testimonials + results
- **FAQ** — Address buyer hesitations ("What's your refund policy?" "How long do I have access?")
- **Guarantee** — Money-back guarantee (reduces purchase friction)

### For Ecommerce Offers
- **Product Gallery** — High-quality images + descriptions
- **Social Proof** — Reviews, ratings, customer photos
- **Trust Signals** — Shipping info, return policy, security badges
- **FAQ** — "What's the return window?" "How long does shipping take?"

### For All Offers
- **Footer** — Contact info, links, legal (privacy, terms)

## Proposed Section Order (Standard Funnel)

The order below works for most offers. Adjust if needed:

1. **Hero** — Hook + value proposition
2. **Problem / Transformation** *(optional)* — Reframe the customer's pain point → show what's possible
3. **Main Value** — How it works / Service description / What's inside
4. **Features / Social Proof / Process** — Builds confidence (varies by offer type)
5. **Objection Handling** — Pricing, FAQ, Guarantee (removes doubt)
6. **Final CTA** — Strong call-to-action ("Let's get started")
7. **Footer** — Contact, links, legal

## Section Decision Checklist

Before adding **any** section, ask:

- [ ] Does this section answer a real question your buyer has?
- [ ] Does this section increase trust or reduce objections?
- [ ] Will removing it hurt your conversion rate?
- [ ] Can this info go somewhere else (like FAQ)?

**If all are "no," remove the section.**

## Sections to SKIP (For Now)

- **Detailed documentation** — Goes in onboarding/help docs, not marketing site
