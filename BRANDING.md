## The BAV 48 Framework & Questionnaire

The BrandAsset Valuator (BAV) uses 48 descriptive imagery attributes to help quantify a brand's personality, which maps directly to the 12 Carl Jung archetypes. 

To help identify your or your client's core archetype and motivation, we've developed a questionnaire that uses these attributes alongside contextual examples.

**Tools created:**
- **Questionnaire:** `docs/lead-magnets/bav-archetype-questionnaire.md` (Use this for client interviews).
- **Framework Matrix:** `web_build/lib/bav-48-framework.json` (Source of truth for the app).
- **Scoring Script:** `scripts/archetype-scorer.py` (Local utility).
- **App Scorer:** `web_build/lib/bav-scorer.ts` (TypeScript version for the live site).
