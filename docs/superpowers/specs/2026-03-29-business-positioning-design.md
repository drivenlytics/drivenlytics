**Supporting proof points:**
- Direct-response fundamentals (copy, psychology, NLP-based persuasion)
- Full-stack execution (no handoffs — strategy through to technical build)
- AI-augmented delivery (enterprise-caliber output at faster turnaround)

**Working tagline:**
> "Performance marketing built on fundamentals — delivered at AI speed."

**Note:** Competitive differentiation ("why Drivenlytics over X") requires real competitor market research before it can be sharpened into accurate sales copy. Current positioning is strategically sound but directional — not final messaging.

## 3. Target Audience

Broad by design at this stage. The offer architecture works across:

- **Solopreneurs / coaches / consultants** — building first offer or online presence
- **Early-stage startups** — need positioning, copy, and a converting site
- **Small businesses** — need credible web presence and basic marketing infrastructure
- **Ecom / DTC brands** — need conversion-focused copy, email flows, ads

All benefit from direct-response copy expertise. Entry point is $1K regardless of segment.

## 4. Service Tier Architecture

### Tier 1 — "Launch Ready" ($1,000 one-time)

A complete lead capture machine, built and live in 5–7 business days.

**Deliverables:**
- 1 landing page built on Landingi (copy written + designed + live)
- Lead capture form connected to client's email platform
- 3–5 email welcome sequence (written + loaded in, automated)

**What makes it valuable:** The copy is direct-response caliber — not generic AI output. The psychology and persuasion layer is Lawrence's edge inside a productized offer.

**Optional add-ons:**
- Additional email sequences
- A/B copy variants
- Domain/DNS setup
- CRM integration ($100)

**Natural upsell:** "Now let's build the full site and funnel around this." → Tier 3

---

### Tier 1 — Content Retainer ($1,000/month)

Ongoing AI-assisted content production, Lawrence-reviewed and edited.

**Choose one track:**
- 4 SEO blog posts/month (keyword-informed, conversion-aware CTAs)
- 1 email sequence/month (5–7 emails)
- 8 social media posts + strategy/month

**Note:** This is a volume-driven recurring revenue model. Highest risk of commoditization long-term — requires Lawrence's voice/edit layer to maintain quality differentiation.

---

### Tier 2 — Brand Foundation Kit ($3,000–$5,000 one-time)

The strategic foundation everything else is built on. Not a design package — a thinking package that includes design outputs.

**Deliverables:**
- ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) definition
- Value proposition development
- Offer construction / refinement
- Competitive market analysis
- Brand positioning statement
- Brand voice guidelines (tone, style, vocabulary)
- Visual identity direction (logo, color palette, typography)
- Core messaging: taglines, headline angles, key copy pillars

**Deliverable format:** Brand Foundation Document + visual direction assets

**Pricing rationale:** This is deep strategic work. Lawrence's copy instincts, market analysis, and brand-building experience (e.g., hangtherapy.com 0-to-1) justify $3K minimum. Underpricing this signals commodity thinking.

---

### Tier 3 — Custom / Retainer ($5,000+/month)

Full execution engagements. Scoped per client.

**Services in this tier:**
- Full site builds (Next.js + headless WP)
- Full funnel strategy, architecture, and build
- Email infrastructure setup (SPF/DKIM/DMARC, drip, lifecycle)
- Meta ads — copy, creative direction, hooks, headlines
- SEO strategy + content program
- Server-side tracking setup (GTM, GA4, Meta Pixel, Search Console)
- GTM strategy and market entry planning
- AI/automation workflows ("automated workflows" in client-facing language)
- Dashboard product (GA4 + Search Console reporting for non-technical clients)

## 5. Positioning Notes for Future Refinement

The following require competitor market research before they can be turned into sharp sales copy:

- **Specific competitive claims** — what other agencies/freelancers in this space are doing, where they fall short, how Drivenlytics wins head-to-head

Once competitive research is done, Lawrence will refine the messaging. The strategic skeleton above is intentionally stable — the words on top of it will sharpen with data.
