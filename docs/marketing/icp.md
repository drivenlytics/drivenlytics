# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — Drivenlytics

## The Archetype
**The Hero (with strong Magician tendencies)**  
They are warriors, crusaders, and competitors driven to prove their worth through courageous and difficult action.

## Their Story
The client's story is that of a tough, ambitious competitor who refuses to accept defeat. They may be facing a formidable enemy (a shifting economy, a ruthless competitor, or a plateau in their own scaling efforts). They are on a quest to become as strong, competent, and powerful as they are capable of being. They don't just want to survive; they want to conquer.

## Demographics & Psychographics
- **Profile:** Ambitious founders, high-level coaches, and entrepreneurial CEOs.
- **Traits:** Highly competitive, disciplined, and focused.
- **Worldview:** Likely view business as an athletic contest or a battlefield.
- **Skepticism:** Deeply skeptical of empty marketing hype.
- **Trust Factors:** Will only trust definitive experts (**Sages**) who can provide them with the innovative breakthroughs (**Magic**) required to scale.
