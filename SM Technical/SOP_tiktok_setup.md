# SOP: TikTok Account Setup for US Organic Reach

## 1. Does It Have to Be a Mobile Phone?

No, but with an important caveat. In 2026, the TikTok algorithm is heavily biased toward **Mobile Metadata**. When you post from a phone, the app sends "human" signals including battery level, screen brightness, and accelerometer data (hand tilt).

**Desktop Posting (via AdsPower + Proxy)**
- Works for reaching a US audience
- Higher likelihood of staying in "low-view jail" (200–500 views) — the algorithm treats it as a business upload, not a human creator
- Best for consistency, harder to go viral

**Mobile Posting**
- Where 1M+ view videos happen
- The algorithm rewards organic human metadata
- Required for maximum organic share potential

## 2. The "Account Birth Certificate" Rule

The US version of TikTok (under USDS "Project Texas" rules) is a **separate app build** from the Vietnamese version.

**The Trap:** Creating an account on a Vietnamese phone and then logging in via a US proxy stamps the account with a Vietnamese Origin. Changing the Home Region afterward is extremely difficult.

**The Rule:** The account must be *born* in a US environment — US SIM, US App Store download, US-based email.

---

## 3. The Canada Mistake — Avoid It

Canada and the US are now entirely separate **data silos** for TikTok.

- A Canadian SIM or Canadian proxy routes organic shares to Toronto, not the US
- For Hang Therapy's target audience (US health-tech-longevity), use **US-only proxies and accounts**

## Execution Plan: Managing from Vietnam

### Step 1 — The US Birth (via a Friend in the US)

Have a US-based contact create the account. Requirements:

- Phone with a **US SIM card**
- TikTok downloaded from the **US App Store** (requires a US Apple ID or Google Account)
- Sign up using a **US-based email** (e.g., Gmail created in the US)
- Post one short video (5-second clip is fine) and let the account sit for **24 hours**

### Step 2 — Desktop Management via AdsPower

Once you have the login credentials:

1. Set up a browser profile in **AdsPower**
2. Assign a **Static US Residential Proxy** (recommended providers: IPRoyal or Bright Data — select "Static Residential")
3. Log in to **TikTok.com** — this is your US Office for content management

### Step 3 — Posting Strategy

**Uploading:** Post Hang Therapy videos via the AdsPower desktop setup.

**The Human Touch:** Every few days, spend ~10 minutes scrolling the For You Page inside your AdsPower browser. Like a few US-based health and wellness videos. This signals to the algorithm that a real person in the US is using the account, not just a bot uploading files.
